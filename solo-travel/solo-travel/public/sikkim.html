Safety and Emergency Information

Emergency Contacts:

  • Gangtok Police Station: +91-3592-202022
  • Emergency Ambulance: 108
  • Tourist Helpline: 1363

Safety Tips for Solo Travelers:

  • Keep your identification and travel documents secure and carry photocopies.
  • Avoid remote areas alone, especially after dark.
  • Stay connected with someone through your phone and inform them of your plans.
  • Be mindful of the mountain weather and carry necessary warm clothing.
  • Carry a basic first-aid kit and any necessary medication.
