Safety and Emergency Information

Emergency Contacts:

  • Kutch Police Station: +91-2832-250206
  • Emergency Ambulance: 108
  • Tourist Helpline: 1363

Safety Tips for Solo Travelers:

  • Stay hydrated and prepare for the desert climate; wear sun protection.
  • Travel with a group when visiting remote areas and avoid venturing out alone at night.
  • Respect the local culture and customs, especially in village areas.
  • Carry a basic first-aid kit and any necessary medication.
