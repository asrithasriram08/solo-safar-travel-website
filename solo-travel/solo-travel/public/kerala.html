Safety and Emergency Information

Emergency Contacts:

  • Kerala Police: 112
  • Ambulance: 108
  • Tourist Helpline: 1800-425-4747

Safety Tips for Solo Travelers:

  • Carry your identification and travel documents securely.
  • Avoid venturing into unknown areas at night.
  • Stay hydrated and carry sunscreen while exploring during the day.
  • Try to stay connected with friends or family while traveling.
  • Be respectful of local culture and traditions.
