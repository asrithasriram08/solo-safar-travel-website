Safety and Emergency Information

Emergency Contacts:

  • Manali Police Station: +91-1902-252326
  • Emergency Ambulance: 108
  • Tourist Helpline: 1363

Safety Tips for Solo Travelers:

  • Always keep your identification and travel documents secure and carry photocopies.
  • Avoid venturing into remote areas alone, especially after dark.
  • Inform someone about your travel plans and stay connected through your phone.
  • Be cautious of extreme weather conditions, especially in mountain areas.
  • Carry a basic first-aid kit and any necessary medication.
