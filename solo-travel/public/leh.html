Safety and Emergency Information

Emergency Contacts:

  • Ladakh Police: 112
  • Ambulance: 108
  • Tourist Helpline: 1800-11-1363

Safety Tips for Solo Travelers:

  • Carry altitude sickness medication and stay hydrated.
  • Avoid overexertion on high passes like Khardung La.
  • Dress in layers to adapt to sudden weather changes.
  • Inform someone about your travel plans and stay connected.
  • Respect local customs and the environment.
