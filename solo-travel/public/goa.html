    Safety and Emergency Information

    Emergency Contacts:

    • Goa Police Station: +91-832-2424001
    • Emergency Ambulance: 108
    • Tourist Helpline: 1363

    Safety Tips for Solo Travelers:

    • Always carry identification and keep your documents secure.
    • Avoid isolated areas alone, especially after sunset.
    • Stay hydrated and carry sunscreen for beach outings.
    • Keep someone informed about your travel plans and check-in regularly.
    • Be aware of local customs and respect the Goan culture.
